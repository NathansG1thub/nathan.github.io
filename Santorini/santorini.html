About This Package

Perched on the rim of an ancient volcanic caldera, Santorini is one of the world's most iconic destinations. This 7-night package places you in the heart of Oia, where cave-cut suites and infinity pools overlook one of the most photographed sunsets on the planet. Every detail is taken care of — from your airport transfer on arrival to a private guided tour of the island's archaeological highlights.

Whether you spend your days exploring the black-sand beaches of Perissa, sailing the caldera on a catamaran, or simply watching the world drift past from a clifftop terrace with a glass of local Assyrtiko wine — Santorini delivers a holiday you will return from changed.

Your Itinerary

  1. 1

    Arrival & Welcome

    Your private transfer whisks you from Santorini Airport to your clifftop hotel in Oia. Settle in, freshen up, and spend your first evening exploring the winding lanes and watching the legendary sunset.

  2. 2

    Caldera & Akrotiri Archaeological Site

    Morning guided tour of the Minoan ruins at Akrotiri — the "Pompeii of the Aegean". Afternoon free for a dip at Red Beach, one of the island's most dramatic volcanic shorelines.

  3. 3

    Catamaran Sailing Day

    A full-day catamaran cruise around the caldera, stopping at the hot springs of Palea Kameni, the white village of Thirassia, and a prime swimming spot. Lunch and drinks served on board.

  4. 4

    Fira & Wine Tasting

    Stroll the cobblestone paths of Fira, the island's capital. In the afternoon, visit a local winery for a guided Assyrtiko tasting overlooking the vineyards.

  5. 5

    Perissa Black Sand Beach

    A relaxed beach day at Perissa. Sunbeds and umbrellas are included, and the beachfront tavernas serve fresh grilled octopus and cold Mythos beer in abundance.

  6. 6

    Free Day

    Your day, your way. Hire a quad bike and explore the island independently, book a spa treatment at the hotel, or return to a favourite spot from earlier in the week.

  7. 7

    Departure

    Enjoy a final breakfast with caldera views before your private transfer back to the airport. Safe travels.
